ABOUT.

Elusive Parts has been created in response to requests from car owners to help solve the challenge of finding spares, combined with a common goal of preserving our heritage and keeping our classics on the road.  

We know the process of finding the right spare part can be frustrating and time-consuming, often leading to your classic car being out of action for long periods and causing you to miss events, races and other opportunities. Elusive Parts aims to make that process faster and easier than ever – helping you get back behind the wheel more quickly.

We employ state of the art computer aided design (CAD), 3D scanning and printing technology to produce high-quality, customised parts in a matter of days.

Tell us about your part.

Please fill out the 'help me get my part' form so that we can review it.

We'll be in touch.

After we receive your form we'll let you know if we can make the part and follow up with further information and questions we may have.

We'll compile the digital part data.

Depending on the information you've provided we'll adopt different methods to prepare this data. If you have a physical version of the part available we'll consult with you about the best way for us to 3D scan it, either by paying you a visit or you sending it to us. This will be the most rapid way for us to reproduce your part. Alternatively we will consult with you about dimensions and look to provide a prototype.

We'll provide you with a quote for manufacture.

We will evaluate the manufacturing cost of the part once we have finalised the part data and provide you with a formal quote.

We'll make and ship your part.

We're currently looking to expand our parts catalogue so if you have any additional rare parts available for scanning please let us know. While we perfect our process, we are offering significant discounts to customers who agree to pilot our parts and share their experience.

We look forward to the future of classic parts manufacturing, preserving our heritage and keeping our classics on the road.
